Clinic+

About
A technology system that optimizes clinic management and improves patient's clinic experience.

Duration
MAR - JUN, 2018
Type
Individual Project

My Role
UI/UX Designer

Problem

Clinical patient and consumer satisfaction is tied to the waiting time. Studies have shown that long wait time and short consultation duration lead to dissatisfaction among patients. Research shows that patients are more likely to be dissatisfied if their waiting time is more than 30 minutes.

Research

The process of patients’ clinical experience usually involves registration, pre-consultation, consultation, appointment, appointment and payment. The results of a research conducted in a primary care clinic over the period of four weeks showed that more than half of the patients were registered within 15 minutes (53%) and the average total waiting time from registration to seeing a doctor was 41 minutes (Ahmad, Kahairatul, Farnaza).

The typical clinical experience of the wait time is already more than 1 hour, if actual consultation time or other medical assessments duration are added, it could be result in 2 hours of total clinical experience time.

Target Audience

The primary target audience and potential stake holders are clinic staff and clinic clients. A system that improves the efficiency of the clinic work flow benefits both parties.

Solution

Using web technologies to create tools that improve clinic communication efficiency and improve patient’s satisfaction. An accessible app that allow patients to make appointments easily and oversee detailed information of their appointments such as dates, wait time, required materials, recommended tasks etc.. The apps in the clinic will allow the patients to check in easily and communicate patient’s information throughout the clinic systems.

Objectives

  • - Improve clinical communication efficiency
  • - Minimize patient wait times
  • - Improve patient satisfaction
  • - Create easily accessible tools for clinical information
