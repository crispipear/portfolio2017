Eatlixir

About
Eatlixir is a concept design on a public platform that resolves unhealthy eating habits. The website provides medicinal diet information inspired by Chinese herbology studies.

Problem

There is an unhealthy eating phenomenon going on in the world today. Some people consume too much junk food and some people often skip meals. These unhealthy eating habits often causes health problems such as indigestion, loss of sleep quality and loss of appetite. Unhealthy eating habits can also lead to major problems such as obesity, heart diseases, strokes and more.

Overall Purpose

Study has shown that medicinal diet is capable of subtly improving human body health conditions. In addition, the Chinese herbology studies believe that some food ingredients have their own characteristics and perform different tasks on the human body. Users will be able to find information from Eatlixir that relieves health problems.

Audience

  • Individuals with health problems
  • Individuals looking to improve their diet or eating habits
  • Nutritionists, doctors
  • General public interested looking for recipes

Objectives

  • Engage audience to improve eating habits
  • Provide useful information and guidance to medicinal diet
  • Help users relieve health problems through diet
  • Reduce junk food consumption

Anticipated Features

  • Medicinal food ingredients information directory
  • Medicinal diet recipes directory
  • Personal body health test
  • Personal blog post system
