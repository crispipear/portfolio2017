Jovian Planets

Introduction:

Jovian Planets is an astronomy infographic that summarizes the key characteristics of Gas Giants/Jovian Planets in the Solar System.

Type:

Graphic Design - Infographic
Web Design/Development

Problem: Some of the astronomy textbooks and website resources give a lot of details on specific astronomical terms. Some audience needs to spend extra effort and time to understand the terms.

Design Concept:

- Display the main characteristics of the planets: name origins, composition, size relative to earth, and distance.
- use only light blue in text to highlight/emphasize specific terms.
- use simple shapes illustrating the four planets.
- use colors that capture the characteristics of the planets on the illustrations.
